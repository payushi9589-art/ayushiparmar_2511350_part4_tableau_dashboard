### Chart Selection Justification



##### 1\. Sales Trend View





**Business Question**



How are sales changing over time?



Chart Type - Line Chart



Why This Chart?



Line charts effectively display trends and patterns over time.

Fields Used

X-Axis: Order Date (Month)

Y-Axis: Sales

Design Principle Applied

Trend visualization and temporal analysis.

Mistake Avoided

Avoided pie charts and categorical charts for time-series data.

\---





##### 2\. Regional Performance View





**Business Question**



Which regions contribute the most profit?



Chart Type - Horizontal Bar Chart



Why This Chart?



Bar charts allow quick comparison across regions.

Fields Used

Region

Profit

Design Principle Applied

Easy comparison and ranking.

Mistake Avoided

Avoided overly complex visualizations for straightforward comparisons.

\---





##### 3\. Category Profitability View





**Business Question**





Which categories and sub-categories generate the most profit?



Chart Type - Horizontal Bar Chart



Why This Chart?



Provides clear ranking and comparison of profitability.

Fields Used

Sub-Category

Profit

Design Principle Applied

Sorted ranking for easier interpretation.

Mistake Avoided

Avoided unsorted charts that make comparisons difficult.

\---





##### 4\. Return Analysis View





**Business Question**



Which categories experience higher return activity?



Chart Type - Stacked Bar Chart





Why This Chart?



Shows category-level return distribution clearly.

Fields Used

Category

Return Flag

Design Principle Applied

Category comparison and contribution analysis.

Mistake Avoided

Avoided cluttered tables that are difficult to interpret visually.

\---





##### 5\. Discount vs Profit View





**Business Question**



How do discounts impact profitability?



Chart Type - Scatter Plot





Why This Chart?



Scatter plots are ideal for identifying relationships between two numerical variables.

Fields Used

Discount

Profit

Design Principle Applied

Relationship and correlation analysis.

Mistake Avoided

Avoided aggregated charts that hide variability.

\---





**Dashboard-Level Design Principles**

Visual Hierarchy

KPIs are positioned at the top for immediate visibility.

Consistent Formatting

Currency and percentage formatting are applied consistently.

Minimal Clutter

Only relevant charts and filters are included.

Interactive Filtering

Dashboard filters allow users to focus on specific business segments.

Readability

Charts use clear titles, labels, and appropriate sizing.

Decision Support

Every visualization directly supports a business decision or performance evaluation.
